## 1. News
- **[Oct 2024]**: x-RAGE paper is now available on arXiv.

## 2. Highlights
1. 36 unique gestures with its variations.
2. Recorded by 6 subjects in 6 different scenarios.
3. Recorded in 3 states (sitting, standing, walking).
4. Recorded 8064 samples in total.
5. Recorded with EVK4 at 1280x720 pixels resolution.
